ReadMind Me

Keep track of the books you own

Have you ever bought the same book twice? Can't remember if you bought the latest volume of your favorite comic series? ReadMind Me is a mobile app that helps you keep track of the books you own. Just enter the ISBN or title/author to add it to your library. You can also schedule reminders to notify you when to make your next book purchase.

Wireframes

Colors, Typography, and Icons

The color palette was designed around blue as the primary color. The secondary color is used as an accent to draw the user's attention to take action as seen in the buttons and selection controls.
The Open Sans typeface was chosen for its clarity and modern look.
Each icon uses solid shapes so that they are easily recognizable.
